# TRON: CyberGrid Sentinel 🛡️

> Enter the Grid. Scan Smart. Pay Safe.

## The Problem
UPI QR scams are costing Indians crores every year. Normal scanners like GPay or Paytm just open the payment page. They never tell you *who* you’re actually paying or *why* a QR is dangerous.

Users are flying blind in the Grid.

## Our Solution: 4 Power Checks
CyberGrid Sentinel is a TRON-inspired UPI QR scanner that acts as your Grid Scanner. Before you pay, it runs 4 security protocols:

| Level | Power Check | Stops This Scam |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **VPA Cracker** | Shop says "Reliance" but VPA is `random123@paytm` |
| 2 | **Merchant Match** | QR says "MSEB Bill" but name doesn’t match board |
| 3 | **Amount Trap** | Pre-filled ₹5000 on fake "refund" QRs |
| 4 | **QR Type** | Dynamic QRs sent for refunds - 99% fraud |

Instead of just blocking scams, we *teach* users why a QR is risky.
